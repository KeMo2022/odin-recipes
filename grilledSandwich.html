Spicy Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Have yourself some delicious out of this world Spicy Grilled Sandwich.

Ingredients

Use the following ingredients to create for yourself an amazing lunch.

  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 roma(plum) tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 small onion, chopped
  • 1 Jalapeno pepper, chopped

Steps

Take the following steps to ensure that your lunch is well prepared and served:

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat a large skillet over low heat. Spread butter or margarine onto one side of 2 slices of bread.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place both pieces buttered side down in the skillet. Lay a slice of cheese on each one, and top with slices of tomato, onion and jalapeno.

  3. Step 3

    4. Butter one side of the remaining slices of bread, and place on top buttered side up.

  4. Step 4

    5. Flip and fry until brown on the side, when the bottom of the sandwiches are toasted.
