Spicy Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

It's time to have ourselves some lunch, and this time, we will be having some Spicy Grilled Cheese Sandwich. These sandwiches will take 2 mins for preparation, 3 mins to cook, so a total of 5 mins.This recipe will yield 2 sandwiches, which is perfect for two people. These delicious sandwiches with melted cheese crunched inside them originated from the great USA, all the way back in the early 1900s. Unlike the pancakes we had for breakfast a few hours ago that were high in calories, our lunch today does not contain much calories, only up to 352 colories. These sandwiches are rich in protein, carbs, calcium, sodium plus some more. That will definetely be more than enough to recharge ourselves, so that we can resume the day.

Ingredients

Use the following ingredients to create for yourself an amazing lunch.

  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 4 slices white bread
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 1 roma(plum) tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 small onion, chopped
  • 1 Jalapeno pepper, chopped

Steps

Take the following steps to ensure that your lunch is well prepared and served:

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat a large skillet over low heat. Spread butter or margarine onto one side of 2 slices of bread.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place both pieces buttered side down in the skillet. Lay a slice of cheese on each one, and top with slices of tomato, onion and jalapeno.

  3. Step 3

    4. Butter one side of the remaining slices of bread, and place on top buttered side up.

  4. Step 4

    5. Flip and fry until brown on the side, when the bottom of the sandwiches are toasted.
