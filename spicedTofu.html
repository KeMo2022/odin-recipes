Breaded, Fried, Softly Spiced Tofu

Description

The day is almost over, it's time to end it with some softly spiced dinner. For this dinner we will be having some breaded, fried and softly spiced tofu. Preparing this meal will take 15 mins and the same goes for cooking it, 15 mins, all together it will take approximately 30 mins. This recipe will yield 4 servings, perfect for 4 people or maybe 2 people, you can eat for four. Tofu originated from China a few centures ago, it contains a mixture of water and soybeans. This is a vegetarian meal, so no meat. The calories for this recipes are lower than the previous two, up to 285 calories. The meal is rich in protein, carbs, fat, sodium, Vitamin B6, and some more. A healthy meal to end the day with.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sage
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Cut pressed tofu into 1/2-inch thick slices; then cut again into 1/2-inch wide sticks. Place tofu in a bowl, and pour broth over the top. Set aside to soak.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a seperate bowl, stir together flour, yeast, salt, pepper, sage, and cayenne.

  3. Step 3

    4. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove tofu sticks from broth, and squeeze most (but not all) of the liquid from them. Roll sticks in breading.(You may have to roll sticks twice to end up with a fairly dry outer layer of breading.)Place tofu in hot oil; fry until crisp and browned on all sides. Add more oil if necessary.
